## About Me
I am a PhD student specializing in computational psycholinguistics and machine learning. My research focuses on human-inspired sentence processing in (large) language models. I am particularly interested in exploring the extent to which these models align with human cognition, with the broader goal of leveraging them to deepen our understanding of human language and language impairments.
